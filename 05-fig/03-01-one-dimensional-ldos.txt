SSH model with additional nearest-neighbour intraorbital hopping $-t$. [] This model shows that the topological state is delicate. Indeed, any nearest-neighbour intraorbital pairing model gives rise to a zero-energy state with density in the bulk. a) Lattice cartoon with three sites, showing hopping terms, $v$, $w$ and $t$; local density of states at zero energy of an SSH model with 15 sites: b) Trivial phase: $v>w,t=0$; c) Topological phase: $w>v,t=0$; d) Trivial with hopping: $v>w>t \neq 0$; e) Topological with hopping: $w>v>t \neq 0$; f) Trivial with strong hopping: $v>t>w$; g) Topological with strong hopping: $w>t>v$; h) Trivial with stronger hopping: $t>v>w$; i) Topological with stronger hopping: $t>w>v$.

Phase diagram of the SSH model in the normal state with $\mu/w=0$. The density of states are plotted as a function of the applied bias energy $\omega$ and intracell, interorbital hopping parameter $v/w$. The topological transition occurs at $v/w=1$. The effect of the chemical potential is to shift the phase boundary, whereas the impurity shifts the phase boundary on a single site, which we observe as a faint line in the density of states, shifted by $V=10.28$.

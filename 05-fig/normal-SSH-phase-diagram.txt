Phase diagram of the SSH model in the normal state with $\mu/w=0$. The density of states are plotted as a function of the applied bias energy $\omega$ and intracell, interorbital hopping parameter $v/w$. The topological transition occurs at $v/w=1$ and notice the faint density at $\omega=0$ below the transition point, due to the edge states. The effect of the chemical potential is to shift the phase boundary, whereas a small impurity shifts the phase boundary on a single site, a large impurity coupling $V=10.28$ gaps out any modes on the middle site.

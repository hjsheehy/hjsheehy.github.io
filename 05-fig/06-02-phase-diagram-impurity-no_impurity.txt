The phase diagrams for an SSH model with multiorbital, equal-spin superconductivity interaction and no impurity. Two initial pairing fields are given to the self-consistent loop: non-unitary triplet and equal-spin triplet. The solution with the lowest free energy is selected. We observe that the mean-field spin-sectors are equal $\overline{\Delta_{v/w}^{\uparrow\uparrow}}=\overline{\Delta_{v/w}^{\downarrow\downarrow}}$, hence the equal-spin triplet theory is favoured.
